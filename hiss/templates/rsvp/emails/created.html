What should you bring?

  • Student ID (required)
  • Laptop, charger, dongles
  • Phone charger
  • Pillow / blanket
  • Hygienic essential
  • Ethernet cable (optional, but encouraged)
  • Hardware for your hack (optional)

What should you NOT bring?

  • Pets (no matter how cute they are)
  • Weapons
  • Soldering Irons
  • Anything you wouldn't bring on a plane
  • Behavior that doesn't follow the MLH Code of Conduct

We highly encourage you to download any software/tools you may need before the hackathon to take the initial load off our systems. :) Bringing an ethernet cable will also help!

Other Important Notes

Please join the HowdyHack 2019 Slack Workspace where you can find important announcements during the event and interact with participants, mentors, and organizers.

Also join the HowdyHack 2019 Facebook Group where you can find teammates, brainstorm ideas, and meet members from different student orgs!
